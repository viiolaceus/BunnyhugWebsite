Moonglow Bay

Moonglow Bay is set along the Eastern Canadian coastline during the 1980s and offers players a diverse and emotionally charged story, complemented by a relaxing, slice-of-life fishing RPG.
You play as a rookie angler, struggling to fulfil your partner’s final wish by keeping a business afloat in this fishing town afraid to fish.

This is our debut game and we're over the moon to be sharing it with you :)
